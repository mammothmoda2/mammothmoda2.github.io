MammothModa

A Unified AR-Diffusion Framework for Multimodal Understanding and Generation

GitHub Tech Report
Experimental Highlights
MammothModa2 is built around a Mixture-of-Experts DiT backbone that unifies multimodal understanding and generation within a single AR-Diffusion architecture.
Upload assets/highlight_moe_dit.png (MoE DiT architecture / expert ablation).
MoE DiT Architecture

First MoE-based DiT video generator with fine-grained experts that specialize over motion, content, and style, enabling scalable capacity without sacrificing efficiency.

Upload assets/highlight_unified.png (unified multimodal architecture diagram).
Unified Multimodal Design

A single AR-diffusion framework that supports text-to-image, image editing, and text-to-video generation with shared representations and training recipes.

Upload assets/highlight_benchmark.png (benchmark scores / latency comparison table).
Efficient & Strong on Public Benchmarks

MammothModa2.5 (20B) achieves 129s video latency on our benchmark setup while delivering competitive VBench2 scores across creativity, commonsense, controllability, human fidelity, and physics.
